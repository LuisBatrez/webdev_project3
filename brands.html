My Favorite Clothing Brands

Some of my current favortite clothing brands and peices I look out for while thrifting.

South Pole

One of the most popular y2k brands for a good reason. This brand has amazing jeans and the occasional good tops.

Akademiks

This brand has amazing jeans that are different from any other brand with their designs.

Fubu

By far one of my favorite brands, their jeans and tops are both amazing with diverse colors and designs.

Oakley

Has some of the coolest accessories like bags, belts, and glasses.
