My Favorite Clothing Brands

Some of my current favortite clothing brands and peices I look out for while thrifting.

South Pole. One of the most popular y2k brands for a good reason. This brand has amazaing jeans and the occasional good tops.

Akademiks. this brand has amazing jeans that are different from any other brand with their designs.

Fubu. By far one of my favorite brands, their jeans and tops are both amazing with diverse colors and designs.

Oakley. Has some of the coolest accessories like bags, belts, and glasses

Quiksilver. Their older stuff is cool but their modern stuff kinda sucks.

Tapout. When most people think of y2k, they think of a tapout shirt. Their clothes usually always has cool designs.

Enyce. This brand gave me some of my favorite hoodies and shirts but usually whenever I do find it I leave it.
